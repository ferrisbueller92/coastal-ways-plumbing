Recommended Solutions

Prioritised by impact. Each solution is mapped to a specific pain point Beau faces and includes cost, time to set up, and the result.

PRIORITY 1 — Do this week

Google Business Profile

When someone searches "plumber near me" or "plumber central coast", Google shows the Map Pack first — before any website. Without a GBP listing, Coastal Ways is invisible to the #1 way people find local plumbers.

Free 30 min setup Highest impact

Result: Beau appears in Google Maps when Central Coast residents search for a plumber. Reviews accumulate here and directly drive new business.

PRIORITY 2 — Do this week

Job Management: ServiceM8 over AroFlo

Beau's business plan considers AroFlo but flags it as "may be overkill for a one man business." We agree. ServiceM8 is purpose-built for Australian solo tradies and does everything Beau needs at half the cost.

PRIORITY 3 — Do this fortnight

Xero Accounting Setup

The business plan already includes Xero. The key is setting it up correctly from day one — chart of accounts for a plumbing business, supplier accounts for Reece and Walsh (arrears tracking), strata invoicing workflow, and GST compliance.

  • Chart of accounts tailored to plumbing trades
  • Supplier accounts: Reece Plumbing, Walsh Plumbing (end-of-month arrears)
  • Invoice templates branded to CWP
  • Bank feed connected to business account
  • Receipt scanning via Hubdoc (included with Xero)
  • ServiceM8 → Xero auto-sync for invoices
  • BAS prep automated quarterly
$29-50/month (Xero plan) 3-4 hours setup Already in plan

Result: Emma and Tim can see financial position any time. BAS prep takes hours not days. Receipts scanned from phone. No shoeboxes of paper invoices.

PRIORITY 4 — Month 1

Branded Email

Currently using enquirebpplumbing@outlook.com. A branded email (beau@coastalwaysplumbing.com) signals professionalism and builds trust — especially important for strata managers and real estate agents who deal with multiple tradies daily.

Free (Zoho Mail) or $7.20/mo (Google Workspace) 30 min setup
PRIORITY 5 — Month 1

Automated Review Requests

Google reviews are the single biggest factor in local search ranking after GBP setup. ServiceM8 can automatically send a "How did we go?" text after each job with a direct link to leave a Google review. Beau's existing customers already give great feedback — this just captures it where it matters.

Included in ServiceM8 15 min setup

Result: 5-star reviews accumulate on Google. Each review makes Coastal Ways rank higher in "plumber near me" searches. Compounds over time — by month 6, Beau could have 30+ reviews while competitors have 5.

PRIORITY 6 — Month 2

Social Media: Before & After Content System

Beau already takes photos of his work. A simple system: snap a before photo when arriving, snap an after photo when done. These get posted to Facebook/Instagram with branded templates — zero extra effort, consistent content that shows Beau's quality workmanship.

  • Before/after photo pairs from ServiceM8 job records
  • Branded template overlay (CWP logo, colours, "Coastal Ways Plumbing" watermark)
  • Posted to Facebook + Instagram 2-3x per week
  • Builds portfolio that speaks for itself
Free Ongoing — 5 min per post Validate — does Beau take job photos now?
PRIORITY 7 — Month 3+

AI-Assisted Quoting

As Beau builds a history of jobs and costs in ServiceM8, we can create a quoting assistant that suggests prices based on job type, materials, and time. This reduces the mental load of pricing every job from scratch and ensures consistent, profitable quotes.

  • Job history analysis — "similar blocked drain jobs have averaged $X"
  • Materials cost calculator with Reece/Walsh pricing
  • Travel time factored by suburb
  • Suggested quote range with margin protection
Custom build — free (pro bono) Needs 3+ months of job data first Validate — is quoting a pain point?
PRIORITY 8 — Month 3+

Online Booking Page

ServiceM8 includes an online booking widget that can be embedded on the website. Customers can request a booking directly — selecting service type, preferred time, and adding photos of the issue. Reduces phone tag and captures leads 24/7.

Included in ServiceM8 1 hour setup
PRIORITY 9 — Month 6+

Maintenance Contract System

The business plan identifies maintenance contracts as an opportunity for recurring revenue. Once Beau has 20+ direct customers, we can build a simple system: annual plumbing check for $X, reminder sent automatically, scheduled in ServiceM8. Turns one-off customers into recurring revenue.

Free — uses existing tools Needs customer base first Validate — is this something Beau wants?

Implementation Timeline

A phased approach — get the high-impact items running first, then layer on automation as the business grows.

Week 1: Foundation

Website live + domain connected. Google Business Profile created. ServiceM8 trial started. Branded email set up.

Week 2-3: Core Systems

ServiceM8 configured (job types, quote templates, invoice templates). Xero set up with chart of accounts. ServiceM8 → Xero sync connected. Bank feeds live.

Month 2: Growth Layer

Automated review requests turned on. First before/after photos posted. Facebook + Instagram pages active. Hubdoc receipt scanning for Reece/Walsh invoices.

Month 3-6: Optimisation

Online booking embedded on website. AI quoting suggestions (once job data exists). Maintenance contract templates. Local directory listings.

Month 6+: Scale

Review data informs marketing (what services get best reviews?). SEO content based on search data. Financial reporting automated for IBA compliance. Ready for apprentice onboarding if needed.
