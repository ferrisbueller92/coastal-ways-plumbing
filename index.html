About Us

10+ years of quality plumbing across Sydney and the Central Coast

With over a decade of hands-on experience, Coastal Ways Plumbing was founded on a simple idea: do the job right, every time. From 8 years specialising in high-end renovation and maintenance services throughout Sydney, to deep local knowledge across the Central Coast, we bring a level of care and precision that sets us apart.

We're not just here to fix the problem in front of us. We take the time to explain what's going on, what the options are, and what we'd recommend — honestly. If something needs doing now to save a bigger job later, we'll tell you.

Coastal Ways is backed by specialist equipment that most plumbers don't carry. Thermal imaging for hidden leaks, electronic pipe locators, CCTV drain cameras and high-pressure jetters mean faster diagnosis, less guesswork, and better results.
