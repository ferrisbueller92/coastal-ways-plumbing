HOW TO CHOOSE

It's an easy yes/no

There's no wrong pick — each is fully on-brand. Tell us which one angle feels most like Coastal Ways (or mix-and-match — "Concept 4's headline with Concept 1's photo"). We refine the winner, then deliver it in both ad sizes plus a version for Facebook/Instagram.

Idea
Rotate 2–3 of these across the year so the ad never goes stale.

What happens next

  1. Emma picks an angle (or asks for a tweak).
  2. We refine copy/layout to the final.
  3. We deliver: print-ready PDF (business-card + double sizes) + a digital version for socials.
  4. Confirm the newsletter's exact ad spec, then submit before the issue deadline.

Production notes (for Dave)

  • Sizes: built at AU business-card 90×55mm. Double-size (90×110mm portrait) reflows from the winner. Confirm against the newsletter's rate card before final.
  • Licence number: NSW Lic. 468634C shown on every concept — required by law on licensed-trade advertising.
  • QR: currently points to the bare domain (small, scannable today). For UTM tracking, set up a coastalwaysplumbing.com/n → 301 redirect and swap to qr-shortlink.png.
  • Before printing: physically scan-test the QR off a printed proof, and check colour on the newsletter's stock (CMYK conversion).
