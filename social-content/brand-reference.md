**Voice rules:**
- Short sentences. No fluff.
- First-person plural ("we") or Beau direct ("I").
- Aussie-natural — avoid Americanisms ("reach out", "stoked", "absolutely crushing it").
- No hype words: "game-changer", "crushing", "blessed", "insane".
- No emoji stacks. One per post max (tool, van, tick).

## Caption structure

**Line 1 — Hook (12 words max):** stop-the-scroll
**Line 2–4 — Story (2–3 short sentences):** what happened, where, what you did
**Line 5 — CTA (1 line):** 📞 0402 269 088 or "DM for a quote"
**Line 6 — Hashtags**

**Length target:** under 200 words total. Instagram caps at 2,200 — stay well under.

## Opening hook patterns that work for local trades

- "Called out to [suburb] this morning…" (narrative)
- "3 signs your [thing] is about to fail:" (listicle)
- "Before / after — [service] in [suburb]" (visual proof)
- "This is why you don't [DIY mistake]" (mild contrarian)
- "We don't just [service]. We [broader value]." (positioning)

## Compliance / safety rails

- Never claim to fix things outside Beau's licence scope (e.g. never advise on electrical). 
- Always imply "licensed plumber" not "guaranteed cheapest" (price wars hurt margins).
- No customer faces in photos without explicit permission.
- No customer addresses in captions — suburb only ("Wyong" not "12 Smith St, Wyong").
- No defamation of other trades — stay positive.
