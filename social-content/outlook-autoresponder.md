# Outlook Auto-responder for `coastalwaysplumbing@outlook.com`

**Purpose:** Every customer enquiry gets an instant acknowledgement so they know it landed, and an emergency phone number so they don't feel ignored if Beau is mid-job.

---

## Auto-reply text (copy/paste)

### Subject line
```
We've got your enquiry — Coastal Ways Plumbing
```

### Body
```
G'day,

Thanks for reaching out to Coastal Ways Plumbing. Your enquiry has landed with us and we'll be back in touch within a few hours during business hours (Mon–Fri 7am–5pm, Sat 8am–2pm).

If it's a plumbing emergency — burst pipe, flood, no water, gas leak — call Beau directly on 0402 269 088. We're available 24/7 for emergencies.

For standard enquiries, we'll reply as soon as we're off the tools.

Cheers,

Beau Petterson
Licensed Plumber · Coastal Ways Plumbing
📞 0402 269 088
🌐 coastalwaysplumbing.com
📍 Central Coast NSW
```

## How to install — Outlook.com (web)

1. Sign into **[outlook.live.com](https://outlook.live.com)** as `coastalwaysplumbing@outlook.com`.
2. Click the **gear icon** (top-right) → **View all Outlook settings**.
3. Left sidebar → **Mail** → **Automatic replies**.
4. Toggle **Turn on automatic replies** ON.
5. **Leave the date range unchecked** (we want this running permanently, not just while away).
6. Paste the subject + body from above into the message field.
7. Check **Send replies only to contacts in my contacts list**: **NO, leave unchecked** — we want auto-reply going to every sender, including new customers.
8. Click **Save** (bottom right).

---

## How to install — Outlook mobile app (iOS/Android)

1. Open the Outlook app, tap the account icon (top-left).
2. Tap the **gear icon** at the bottom.
3. Under "Mail accounts" tap `coastalwaysplumbing@outlook.com`.
4. Tap **Automatic Replies**.
5. Toggle **Automatic Replies** ON.
6. Choose **Reply to everyone**.
7. Paste body from above.
8. Save (top-right).

---

## Important note about this auto-reply

- Outlook.com consumer tier auto-replies go to **every sender**, every time. Some automated systems (newsletters, other auto-responders) will loop — that's unavoidable.
- For new customers, the auto-reply is a win: sets expectation, gives the emergency number, reduces "did they get my enquiry?" follow-ups.
- Beau should still check the inbox daily. The auto-reply doesn't replace reading — it just buys time.

---

## When to turn it OFF

Never, by default. Leave it on permanently. The only time to pause is if Coastal Ways goes on a true shutdown (e.g. Christmas close-down) — in which case swap the message temporarily.
