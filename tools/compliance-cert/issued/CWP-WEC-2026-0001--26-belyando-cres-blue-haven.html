I, Beau Petterson, holding a plumbing licence under the Home Building Act 1989 (NSW) (Licence No. 468634C), certify that on the inspection date specified above I attended the property identified above and inspected the plumbing fixtures at the property. I confirm that, as at the inspection date:

  1. All shower heads at the property have a maximum flow rate of 9 litres per minute.
  2. All internal cold water taps and single mixer taps serving kitchen sinks and bathroom hand basins have a maximum flow rate of 9 litres per minute.
  3. All toilets at the property are dual-flush with a minimum 3-star WELS rating, in accordance with the Water Efficiency Labelling and Standards Act 2005 (Cth).
  4. There are no leaking taps or toilets at the property.
  5. The property is supplied by an individually metered water service.

The property therefore satisfies the water efficiency measures prescribed by clause 10 of the Residential Tenancies Regulation 2019 (NSW) for the purposes of section 39(1)(b) of the Residential Tenancies Act 2010 (NSW).
