Reference · Water bill help after a concealed leak (Central Coast Council)
Central Coast Council can reduce the water bill after a concealed leak (up to 50% of the additional usage, capped at $1,000, one claim per ownership). The customer must apply within 30 days of the bill issue date with a statutory declaration plus a licensed plumber’s report stating: licence number & contact details · that the leak was concealed from view and could not be located by visual inspection · that the failure was not due to faulty plumbing · how the leak was located · details of the repairs · the meter reading at repair. Not available for tenanted, vacant or holiday properties, or for fixture faults (taps, cisterns, hot water systems), pools, irrigation or hoses. This report is designed to carry all of that — tick the statements below only when they’re true.
