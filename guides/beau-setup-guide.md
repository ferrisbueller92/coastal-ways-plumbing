## Step 2: Create a Google account for the business

**What this is:** One Google login that controls everything Google-related for Coastal Ways — your business listing on Google Maps, Google Reviews, YouTube (if you ever want it), and Google Calendar.

**Why it matters:** When someone searches "plumber near me" on their phone, Google shows a map with local businesses. Without a Google Business Profile, you're invisible in that map. This is the #1 way people find local plumbers.

**What to do:**
1. Open your browser and go to **accounts.google.com/signup**
2. Tap **Create account** → choose **For work or my business**
3. Fill in:
   - First name: `Coastal Ways`
   - Last name: `Plumbing`
   - Email: pick something like `coastalwaysplumbing@gmail.com`
   - Password: something strong, write it down somewhere safe
4. Add your phone number (0402 269 088) for recovery
5. Complete the signup

**After you've done this:** Write down the email and password. Send Dave the email address (not the password). You'll use this login for the next step.

## Step 3: Set up Google Business Profile

**What this is:** Your listing on Google Maps. When someone searches "plumber Central Coast" or "plumber near me" from Jilliby, Wyong, Tuggerah — this is what shows up. It's free and it's the single most important thing for getting phone calls from new customers.

**What to do:**
1. Go to **business.google.com** on your laptop (easier on a bigger screen)
2. Sign in with the Google account you just created
3. Tap **Add your business to Google**
4. Search for "Coastal Ways Plumbing" — if it doesn't exist yet, tap **Add your business**
5. Fill in these details:

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|---------------|
| Business name | `Coastal Ways Plumbing` |
| Business category | Start typing `Plumber` and select it |
| Do you have a location customers can visit? | Select **No** (you go to them) |
| Service area | Type `Central Coast` and select the NSW region |
| Phone number | `0402 269 088` |
| Website | `coastalwaysplumbing.com` (we'll connect this shortly) |

6. Google will ask to **verify** your business. Options:
   - **Phone verification** (fastest) — they call or text your number with a code
   - **Postcard** (slower) — they mail a postcard with a code to your address. Takes 5-7 days.
   - Pick phone if it's offered.

7. Once verified, add a few details:
   - **Hours:** Mon-Fri 7am-5pm, Sat-Sun "Emergency calls only"
   - **Business description:** "Licensed plumber serving the Central Coast. 10+ years experience in residential, commercial and emergency plumbing. Leak detection, blocked drains, hot water systems, gas fitting, bathroom renovations. Available 24/7 for emergencies."
   - **Photos:** Upload 3-5 of your best job photos, a photo of your van, and the Coastal Ways logo

**After you've done this:** Your listing will start appearing on Google Maps. It takes 24-48 hours to show up. Every Google Review you get from a customer makes you rank higher.

**What this is:** A Facebook page for Coastal Ways Plumbing (separate from your personal Facebook). Customers can find you, see your work, leave reviews, and message you.

**What to do:**

2. Tap the menu (three lines) → **Pages** → **Create**
3. Fill in:
   - Page name: `Coastal Ways Plumbing`
   - Category: `Plumber`
   - Bio: "Licensed plumber on the Central Coast. Residential, commercial & emergency. Call 0402 269 088."
4. Upload the Coastal Ways logo as the profile picture
5. Upload a cover photo (a job photo or the van works well)
6. Add your phone number, website, and service area in the Page Info section

**After you've done this:** Share the page link with Dave. He'll make sure it's linked from the website.

## Step 7: Instagram Business Account

**What this is:** An Instagram page where you can post before/after job photos. Visual proof of quality work. Instagram is where younger homeowners and property managers look.

**What to do:**
1. Open Instagram → tap your profile → **Settings** → **Account** → **Switch to Professional Account** → choose **Business**
2. If you don't have an Instagram yet, create one:
   - Username: `coastalwaysplumbing`
   - Profile name: `Coastal Ways Plumbing | Central Coast Plumber`
   - Bio: "Licensed plumber. 10+ years experience. Residential • Commercial • Emergency 24/7. Central Coast NSW 📞 0402 269 088"
3. Upload the logo as your profile picture
4. Link to your website in the bio

**Don't stress about posting yet.** Just get it set up. When you start taking before/after photos on jobs, that becomes your content.
